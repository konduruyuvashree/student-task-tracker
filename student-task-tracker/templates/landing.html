What it does

Everything you need to stay on track

➕
Add & Manage Tasks
Create tasks with title, subject, deadline, and priority. Edit or delete anytime with one click.
⚠️
Deadline Reminders
Auto-detects overdue and upcoming tasks and shows a reminder banner so you never miss a submission.
🔍
Subject-wise Filter
Filter your tasks by subject or status instantly. Find exactly what you need in seconds.
🌙
Dark Mode
Toggle between light and dark themes. Your preference is saved automatically for next time.
📊
Live Stats Dashboard
See your total, pending, completed, and due-today counts update in real time as you work.
🐍
Python Flask Backend
Built with Flask and SQLite. Your data is stored persistently — tasks survive app restarts.
